        <div accordion-content="" id="description">
          In this essential meditation book for the 21st century, Osho turns the
          traditional notion of meditation practice on its head. Meditation: The
          First and Last Freedom shows that meditation is not a spiritual
          discipline separate from everyday life in the real world. In essence,
          it is simply the art of being aware of what is going on inside and
          around us. As we acquire the knack, meditation can be our companion
          wherever we are-at work, at play, at rest.<br /><br />Meditation
          contains practical, step-by-step guides to a wide variety of
          meditation techniques selected by and/or created by Osho, including
          the unique OSHO Active Meditations which deal with the special
          tensions of contemporary life. Recognizing that it's almost impossible
          for most people these days just to stop and sit silently, these
          meditations - including the Osho Dynamic Meditation and Osho Kundalini
          Meditation - begin with one or more stages of vigorous physical
          activity. This brings our physical and mental energies to a peak, so
          that the following silence is easy -- leaving us alert, refreshed, and
          newly energized.<br /><br />Newly revised, the perfect text to begin
          or continue exploring the joys of meditation.
