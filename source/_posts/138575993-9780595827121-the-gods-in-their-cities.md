          documented, and all of them exist in multiple copies on Earth.

          mythic guises of the Ray Masters, 14 select great Adepts involved with
          many aspects of Earth life and geomancy since the beginning-including
          the true identity of Jesus, Mary Magdalene, the Virgin Mary, and
          Merlin.

          interactive locales across the Earth exist can inspire confidence,
          even certainty, that reality, and thus our planet, culture, and
          individual human lives, have meaning and are purposeful.
