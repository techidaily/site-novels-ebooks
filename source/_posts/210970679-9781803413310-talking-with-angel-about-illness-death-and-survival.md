          <p>
            Talking with Angel about Illness, Death and Survival is the moving
            story of a young girl battling leukaemia. She eventually realizes
            that she is going to die and receives hope and comfort through
            nightly conversations with her favourite doll, Angel, who helps her
            embrace a new perspective on dying and the possibility that
            consciousness may survive after death. Her fear of death is
            ultimately lifted by her new-found spiritual wisdom and by the
            account of a near-death experience told to her by a young companion,
            as well as by a deathbed vision she has of her deceased
            grand-mother.
          </p>
