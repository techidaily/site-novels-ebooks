          <p>
            Based on the traditional <i>Book of Hours</i> - psalms said daily,
            at set times, by religious communities throughout the world -
            <i>Seasons of the Day</i> reveals the enduring relevance of this
            ancient practice <br />to contemporary living.<br /><br />In
            <i>Seasons of the Day</i>, Sister Stan, knowing that our modern-day
            understanding of time pushes us to our stressed-out limits, passes
            on the monastic ritual to the layperson.<br /><br />Here, as she
            slows us down, she reveals the psalms through her own words, her own
            prayers. Using them, she also guides us tenderly through a four-week
            period, from the silence and mystery of pre-dawn (<i>matins</i>)
            through to the reflective conclusion of the day (<i>compline</i>).
            <br /><br />In a world that can often seem hostile and unfriendly,
            her gentle reflections help the reader find inner peace and
            confidence.<br /><br />A prayer book for today
