          Before the rise of monotheism, the early Hebrews were pagan and God

          spiritual connection to nature and the cycles of life, cannabis,

          the Goddess was thrown out of the Hebrew temple by the monotheists, so

          appears and the Bible is reframed by comparing it to the mythologies
          of neighboring cultures.
