          Particularly focused on those in pain and in need of comfort and

          touching on many facets of our self-ascribed limitations, which with
          patience and love she explores and explodes. Exemplary is her first
          teaching, "Release unnecessary thoughts," and she explains: "When we
          release the mind's clutter, we move beyond the constraints of time.
          The present moment becomes part of an unchanging eternal present Now."
          The focused, incisive and caring nature of <i>108 Teachings</i> makes
          it a perfect work of daily meditations, the practice of which place
          the reader on a unique path of discovery and self-realization.
