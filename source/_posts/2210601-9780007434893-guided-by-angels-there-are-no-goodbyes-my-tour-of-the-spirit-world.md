            No Goodbyes provides reassurance and answers to this and many other
            questions. The main object is to help people go through their
            existence on Earth secure in the knowledge that life continues as an
            adventure towards complete freedom of spirit, so that there's no
            reason whatever to be afraid of death.
          </p>

            angels, or spirit guides. He believes that we are born with
            spiritual connections to one or more angels, and if we want their
            help in life we need to be open to receiving their assistance.
