          tales of the strange and supernatural. From Newburyport to Truro,
          these graveyards often frighten the living, but the dead who rest
          within them have stories to share with the world they left behind.<br />

          Salem, cursing those involved in the infamous witch trials, visitors
          to the Forest Hills Cemetery in Jamaica Plain enjoy an arboretum and a
          burial ground with Victorian-era memorials. One of the oldest
          cemeteries in Massachusetts, Old Burial Hill in Marblehead, has been
          the final resting place for residents for nearly 375 years. Author
          Roxie Zwicker tours the Bay State's oldest burial grounds, exploring
          the stones, stories and supernatural lore of these hallowed places.<br />
