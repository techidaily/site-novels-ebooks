          <p>
            The way the modern stock exchange functions goes back to 1602. The
            horoscope of the creation is still very relevant. The temporary
            aspects in that horoscope reflect the important early developments
            of the stock exchange, for example the early conflicts, fraud and
            manipulation, the invention of short selling, futures and other
            financial instruments the way we use today. Other historical
            developments that affected the functioning of the stock exchange,
            for example the chain of events that led to the American Revolution,
            are also reflected by this chart, as are modern developments, for
            example the bankruptcy of Lehman Brothers, and the following
            crisis.<br />Researching this primal horoscope of the modern stock
            exchange in the light of history clearly shows how important this
            chart still is. In this study is shown how we can use this chart to
            understand future tendencies in the world of finance. It provides an
            important complement to the existing astrological methods to study
            financial dynamics.
