          consequences. He examines ESP, poltergeist disturbances,

          from an electromagnetic perspective. He also reveals how the
          artificial, alien energies we've been introducing into our environment
          shape the way we experience the paranormal.<br /><br /><i
            >Strange Electromagnetic Dimensions</i

          sixth sense similar to that of homing pigeons?<br />
          &nbsp;Are artificial electromagnetic fields, such as those emitted by
          power lines, gradually destroying our health?<br />
          &nbsp;Can being struck by lightning or suffering a severe electric
          shock result in the development of psychic abilities?<br />
          &nbsp; What do scientists make of the fact that ball lightning can
          pass through walls, enter rooms by squeezing through keyholes, and
          display seemingly intelligent behavior?
