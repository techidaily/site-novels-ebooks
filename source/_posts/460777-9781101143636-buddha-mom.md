          In <b>Buddha Mom</b>, Jacqueline Kramer beautifully illuminates the
          ways in which motherhood can be woven with the spiritual life. Drawing

          wisdom traditions from around the world, she offers powerful insights

          a guide to integrating the roles of parent and spiritual being. A
          celebration of all that motherhood can be, <b>Buddha Mom</b> presents
          an inspiring vision of child rearing.
