          life history. As the victim of discrimination, personal vendettas,
          media assassination, libel and attempted murder, Karlén is forced to
          fight for her very being. In the dramatic conclusion to her living
          nightmare, she is shown the karmic background to these events. She
          glimpses fragments of her former life, and begins to understand how
          forces of destiny reach over from the past into the present. With this
          knowledge she is finally free to be herself...And the Wolves Howled is
          the story of one woman's superhuman struggle for truth in the face of
          discrimination and lies.
