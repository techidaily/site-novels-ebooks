          <p>
            <strong>Belief Therapy Volume II:</strong> Finding Freedom in
            Everyday Life exposes a simple thought process to enhance our lives:
            there is nothing wrong with us. We are already okay, there is
            nothing to fix, there never was. It is that simple.
          </p>
          <p>
            Oddly enough, it is often the attempts to fix ourselves that lead to
            our dis-ease.&nbsp;The "problem" is the belief&nbsp;in
            brokenness.&nbsp;The compulsive self-destructive behavior creates
            "evidence" of brokenness, continuing the self-fulfilling prophecy of
            desperation and limitation.&nbsp;&nbsp;
          </p>
          <p>
            Once the belief in our brokenness takes hold, our options become
            limited.&nbsp;We either attempt to fix what was never broken, or we
            give up trying thusly, accepting the belief in our brokenness.
            &nbsp;Subsequently, causing the limiting destructive patterns.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <p>
            By adopting the tenants of <strong>Belief Therapy</strong> and
            recognizing our inherent and pre-existing "goodness," we lose these
            limiting compulsive behaviors and recognize our freedom to choose a
            life of quality.&nbsp;&nbsp;
          </p>
