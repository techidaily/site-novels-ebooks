          How awake are we to our inner being, our true nature? How much
          self-knowledge do we really have?In relation to our intrinsic self, we

          book is an inward one, concerned with strengthening our individuality.
          Based on life-long research, Karsten Massei has created a valuable
          workbook for knowing and healing ourselves. In a series of short
          chapters, he explains the interplay and tensions between the human
          individual and the nature of our 'inner and cosmic child'. Both are
          complex entities but are directly related; both are deeply connected
          with our destiny.Our experiences with our inner child are often still
          in the earliest stages – but cultivating a relationship with her,
          noticing her, holding conversations with her, is vital, and offers us
          ever deepening experiences. As our insights expand, our frailties,
          deficiencies and inner wounds become apparent. The being of the inner
          child wants to educate us to become inwardly truthful and authentic.
          Only honest engagement with the traumas and vulnerabilities of our
          soul will enable a true picture of ourselves to arise.Child of the
          Cosmos contains surprising perspectives arising from the author's
          personal experiences, opening up a clear path of personal development.
          The text is complemented with seven special meditations to assist us
          in engaging with the challenges ahead.
