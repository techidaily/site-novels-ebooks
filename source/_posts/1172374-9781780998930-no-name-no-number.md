          Once in a while somebody steps out of line and decides to live their
          life in a completely different way to the norm. Yoga teacher Hilary
          Carter is such a person. When numbers and number patterns (especially
          the time prompt 11:11) began to appear in her everyday life she
          decided to see what would happen if she used the numbers as signs and
          followed them. Read what happens as Hilary is led by these number
          signs to a ruined convent in the heart of Unesco-protected prehistoric
          France. Why had she been guided to buy it? What was the reason for her
          being in the Dordogne region of France? She travels to Canada, USA,
          Mexico, Turkey and the Ashrams of India in search of answers. All the
