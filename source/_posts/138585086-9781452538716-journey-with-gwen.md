            At twenty-six, a young, married Barbara Puffin had been living

            depression. Mainstream therapy doesnt work.

            starts to make sense. Her new thinking processes are tested by the

            Barbara meets a powerful teacher in another country while online.
            Because of the energetic exchanges, she shifts even further away
            from who she was, and psychic abilities emerge. Barbara knows she
            cant tell Gwen everything, and Gwen is confounded by the shift.
          </p>
          <p>
            As they age, Barbara moves into a caretaker role for Gwen. Barbaras
