          &nbsp;<br /><b>Fear—the most destructive force in the world.</b><br />
          For most of us in the modern world, fear has become so normal that we
          don’t even realize we’re afraid. Instead of living in a state of
          internal peace, we are constantly telling ourselves elaborate stories
          that lead to mental anguish. These stories foster the beliefs that we
          are not enough, that bad things are going to happen to us, or that the
          people we love don’t or won’t love us back. Because we have been
          telling ourselves stories like these for so long, we don’t realize
          that they are stories; we mistake them for facts.<br />
          &nbsp;<br />
          According to bestselling author and Toltec teacher don Miguel Ruiz
          Jr., these stories have one thing in common: they have all been
          poisoned by the arrow of fear.<br />

          the unknown, and how to counter fear with faith and unconditional
          love. Whether you are a longtime student or new to Toltec teachings,
          his expert wisdom will guide you along the path to achieving freedom
          from fear.
