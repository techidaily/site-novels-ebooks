          Earth and humanity are involved in a fragile interplay of physical and
          spiritual relationships, the full spectrum of which can only be
          discerned by higher, finer senses. Based on actual, living discernment
          rather than dogmatic ideas, Karsten Massei reveals how we can unite
          with nature, supporting our inner development and everyday lives. We
          ourselves comprise the path leading to the mysteries of the earth.
          Through delving into nature's secrets, paradoxically we are brought
          back to our own being – our soul-gold, our luminosity, but also to our
          shadow, lower nature. Deep encounters with the earth are thus
          self-encounters.In a series of concise and accessible chapters, Massei
          illumines human characteristics – our senses, the quality of our
          listening, our soul wounds and the possibility for transformation.
          Likewise, he lights up the natural world – plants and animals, but
          also elemental beings, spirits of trees, and the great being of the
          earth, Christ. Amongst a wealth of interrelated themes, the author
          portrays death as the doorway to a new existence, describing the
          relationship of the dead with the earth and humanity and speaking
          movingly of the healing social deed of forgiveness. Founded on
          first-hand research, this book is full of reverence for the hidden
          aspects of life and their significance for personal growth.
