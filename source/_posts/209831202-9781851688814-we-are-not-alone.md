          answer: no. Abundant extraterrestrial life is astrobiological fact and

          the outer reaches of space, it’s on our very doorstep. From methane

          and Darling contend that microbial life is a near certainty both in
          the Solar System and beyond. Using the latest scientific data,
          including from the Phoenix probe, which landed on Mars in 2008, We Are
          Not Alone stands to truly revolutionize our perception of our place in
          the universe.
