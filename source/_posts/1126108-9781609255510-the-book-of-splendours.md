          <span>About the Author</span>
        </h4>
        <p></p>
        <p>
          Alphonse Louis Constant, better know by his pen name
          <b>Eliphas Levi</b>, was a master of the traditional Rosicrucian
          interpretation of the Kabbalah. He was born in France in 1810, and
          through the offices of the parish priest, was educated for the church
          at SaintSulpice. He was later expelled from seminary for teaching
          doctrines contrary to those of the Roman Catholic Church. In 1824 Levi
          began studying the occult sciences, and wrote about magic and the
          Kabbalah for the next three decades. His other books include
          <i>Transcendental Magic</i>, <i>Mysteries of the Qabalah</i>, and
          <i>The Book of Splendours</i>.
        </p>
