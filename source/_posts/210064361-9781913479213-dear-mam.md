          <p>Dear Mam</p>
          <p><br /></p>
          <p>
            This is the true story of a journey from grief to growth through
            writing.
          </p>
          <p>
            It is composed of the letters written by a daughter after the sudden
            death of her beloved Mother in January 2019.
          </p>
          <p>
            The aim of this book is to help others deal with grief or loss of
            any kind, and to encourage readers to connect to themselves and
            others, living or dead, through writing letters.
          </p>
          <p>
            This story is raw and honest, tracing moments of deep sadness and
            confusion, but also times of happy memories and hope for the future.
          </p>
          <p>
            This is a story of love, it comes straight from the heart and is
            honest, intimate and inspirational.
          </p>
          <p><br /></p>
          <p>The Author</p>
          <p><br /></p>
          <p>
            Pauline Burke, the author is originally from Ireland, and has lived
            and worked in Italy as a teacher for many years. She is a Mother
            herself, which she considers to be the greatest lesson and gift of
            her life. In addition to writing, her passions include music, dance,
            and compassionate communication.
          </p>
          <p>
            You can find her at her Facebook page, 'Dystonia Warriors', which
            she created to help all those (including herself) affected by the
            painful movement disorder called Dystonia. Her greatest wish is to
            positively impact others facing any kind of physical, mental,
            emotional and/or spiritual challenge, and she plans to start a new
            letter-writing movement which can connect people and change their
            lives for the better.
          </p>
