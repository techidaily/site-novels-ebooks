          &nbsp;<br /><i>The Language of Lenormand </i>is one of the clearest
          guides ever written for this beloved divination system. Popular in
          Europe for centuries, the Lenormand deck, consisting of thirty-six
          numbered and named cards, has now been embraced in North America by
          cartomancers, tarot enthusiasts, oracle readers, and many others. Each
          card depicts an archetype—a fox, a snake, a coffin, or whip, for
          example—rather than tarot’s somewhat more involved symbology. This
          makes the Lenormand system an easy entry point to divination.<br />
          &nbsp;<br />
          Widely respected authority Erika Robinson believes that Lenormand is
          not just a deck of cards but also a language that, if mastered, can
          help you achieve self-empowerment and the ability to attain the life
          you desire. A longtime English teacher, she helps readers become
          fluent in the language of Lenormand. This clearly written, accessible
          guide to the meanings of each card requires no prior knowledge of
          Lenormand, but is simultaneously a sophisticated book that takes
          Lenormand to the next level, providing greater insights and
          elucidation for those already familiar with it.
