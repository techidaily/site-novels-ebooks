          <p>
            As the last rays of the industrial age finally give way to the full
            force of the information age, a giant leap in the complexity and
            pace of daily life takes place despite the change-fatigue that has
            taken up residence in our corporations, businesses and homes.
            Tolerance levels are shifting to disallow energy-sapping
            step-by-step processes, moving toward accelerated growth through
            iteration and innovation. The result is that the
            change-plateau-change pattern of the past has morphed into unceasing
            transformation.
          </p>

            Social networking, reality media, and the masses of information at
            our fingertips offer a corresponding toolbox for all to use in
            rising to the challenge but theres a catch. For not only do we need
            to stretch even farther to learn the mechanics of these tools, they
            are so powerful that intense focus is required to apply them
            dynamically enough to reap their benefit.
          </p>
          <p>
            This book offers professionals, business leaders, artists,
            entrepreneurs, parents, and students the mental shifts and soft
            skills required to make the leap to everyday transformation. It
            describes how best to approach and wield the new technology toolset
            and ultimately lights a path to flourishing in the new era of
            <i>everything</i>, <i>all the time</i>.
