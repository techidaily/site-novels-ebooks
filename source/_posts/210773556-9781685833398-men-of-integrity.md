          <p>
            Men of Integrity, written by Aaron Porter, is a powerful yet
            intimate work of the heart aimed at men of today's era. Whether you
            are 24 or 74, any man could benefit from the ideas and insights. The
            book is a matter-of-fact accounting of his boyhood and broken
            manhood, which led him, along with many men like him, into a life of
            violence, crime, infidelity, and eventually prison. He explains the
            brokenness of society and how that leads young men to acquire the
            "wrong expectations of God" and, out of disillusionment, begin to
            act out and live life according to their own rules and their
            know-how.
          </p>
          <p>
            "Men of Integrity" is a plea for men to take off the masks of false
            manhood and take accountability for their lives. For any man who has
            found his life has become full of lies about drug abuse, violence,
            or infidelity, this book offers a blueprint, a way out, but also a
            way to live through Jesus Christ and thrive in life as you move
            forward.
          </p>

            and soul.
