          ><br /><br />Many of us have learned to ignore, deny, or even mistrust
          the wise messages our bodies give us. The result is that when trauma
          strikes, a time when we need every aspect of our beings to master the
          challenge, we may find ourselves disconnected from our greatest
          strengths. Suzanne Scurlock-Durana, who has spent thirty years

          hold and how to reconnect with that wisdom. As she shows in this warm,
          compassionate book, the body’s abilities are always available; we must
          simply reconnect with them.<br />
