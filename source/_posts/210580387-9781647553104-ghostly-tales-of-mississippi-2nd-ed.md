        <p>
          <b>Jeff Duke</b> was raised in Tupelo, Mississippi, and began writing
          at an early age. He formulated a love for all things horror after
          spending untold hours in detention with books by Stephen King and H.P.
          Lovecraft. In college, he studied creative writing under Barry Hannah
          at the University of Mississippi and under Dr. Price Caldwell at
          Mississippi State University. Although he mainly writes Southern-style
          “grit lit” these days, he still enjoys writing about horrible things
          that go bump in the night. Jeff currently resides in Austin, Texas,
          with his dog, Boone; his cat, Gabbers; and his wife, Angelica, who he
          still thinks is way out of his league.
