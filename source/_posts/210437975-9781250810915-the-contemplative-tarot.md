            draws deeply on Christian tradition and theology to create a tarot
            practice built on spiritual reflection, prayer, and worship.<br /><br />The
            Tarot has held countless meanings to countless readers over the
            course of its existence, but <i>The Contemplative Tarot</i> offers a
            uniquely Christian interpretation of the cards, including Bible
            verses and meditations on each of their spiritual meanings. Brittany
            provides practical guidance on how to use the cards in a Christian
            context, whether as prompts for personal prayer, daily reminders of
            spiritual truths, or as part of a more structured practice such as
            the Ignatian examen.<br /><br /><i>The Contemplative Tarot</i>
            offers a thoughtful, introspective new interpretation of the Tarot

            worship in your daily life.
