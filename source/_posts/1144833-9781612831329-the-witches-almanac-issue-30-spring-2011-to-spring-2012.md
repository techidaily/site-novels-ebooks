          <p>
            Since 1971, <i>The Witches’ Almanac</i> has been the source of fun,
            wisdom, trivial and magical lore for thousands of readers throughout
            the world. Modeled after the <i>Old Farmer’s Almanac</i>, it
            includes information related to the annual Moon calendar (weather
            forecasts and horoscopes), as well as legends, rituals, herbal
            secrets, interviews, and spells. Although it is an annual
            publication, only 15 percent of the content is time sensitive.
