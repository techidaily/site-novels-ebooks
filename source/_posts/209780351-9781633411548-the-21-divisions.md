            Like all forms of Caribbean Voodoo, practitioners of the 21
            Divisions believe in one God, a distant God that doesn’t get
            involved in human affairs. Followers of this Dominican spiritual

            beings known as Los Misterios.

            relationship issues, heal illness, and much more.
          </p>
