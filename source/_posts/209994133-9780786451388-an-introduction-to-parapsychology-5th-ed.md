            parapsychologists' efforts to explore the authenticity and bases of
            anomalous, apparently paranormal phenomena. It outlines the origins
            of parapsychological research and critically reviews investigations
            of extrasensory perception, psychokinesis, poltergeist phenomena,
            near-death and out-of-body experiences, and the evaluation of
