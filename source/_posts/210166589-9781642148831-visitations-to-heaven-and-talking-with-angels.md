            Would God actually summon someone to Heaven far above the earth, the
            sun, the moon, and all the constellations? Prior to standing on a
            cloud and viewing all of Heaven's beauty and glory, my answer would
            have been an emphatic no. However, I found myself standing on a
            fluffy, impenetrable white cloud, witnessing the beautiful holy city
            called Heaven, and then I saw Him. His face and garment radiated a
            supernatural brilliant light that was much brighter than the morning
            sun coming up over a horizon too magnificent to gaze upon. There is
            no verbal vernacular to adequately describe the exquisite golden
            streets, breathtaking jeweled walls, fragrances incomparable to any
            on earth, and the Heavenly choir singing melodious songs to worship
            and praise the King of kings and Lord of lords. There are nine
            classifications of Angels, all having different assignments. Some
            have four faces, one resembling that of a man and the other three
            faces are the likenesses of animals. Only the Angels assigned to
            mankind sent to provide assistance, directions, encouragement,
            instructions, or to reveal or fulfill the plan of God resemble human
            beings. There is life after death and a Heavenly home where Yeshua
            Hamashia, Jesus the Christ, resides and where He has a prepared a
            residence for all those who love Him.Many personal visitations of
            Angels and that of Heaven are included in this writing. In fact, it
            was an Angel who prompted me to pen this book, and it is my
            expressed hope that your faith will be elevated and that you too
            will experience the supernatural!
