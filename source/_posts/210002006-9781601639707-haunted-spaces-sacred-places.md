          Colorado’s San Luis Valley.The truth behind the legends of the
          mausoleum of the First Qin Emperor in China, home of the Terracotta
          Warriors.The prevalence of modern encounters with ghosts, UFOs, spook
          lights, Bigfoot, and phantom dogs at ancient sacred places. &nbsp;<br />
          With more than 25 photographs and illustrations, this is the ideal

          places, folklore, and unexplained phenomena.
