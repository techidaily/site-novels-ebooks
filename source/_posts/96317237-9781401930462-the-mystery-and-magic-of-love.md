        <p>
          Krishan Chopra, M.D. (1919-2001) was a prominent Indian cardiologist.
          He was head of the department of medicine and cardiology at the
          prestigious Mool Chand Khairati Ram Hospital, New Delhi, for more than
          26 years. Prior to that, he was professor of medicine and chief of
          cardiology at A.F. Medical College, Pune. He was chairman of the Heart
          Care Foundation of India, a national charitable trust engaged in
          continuing medical and public education programs, and was involved in
          professional development in higher education at the University of
          Delhi. He wa also president of Vanaprastha Sansthan, a division of
          Chinmaya Mission, New Delhi, and engaged in public welfare and
          education program for the elderly. Dr. Chopra was actively involved in
          the care and well-being of others throughout his life. The Mystery and
          Magic of Love was his second book, and is a magnificent legacy of
          divine love
