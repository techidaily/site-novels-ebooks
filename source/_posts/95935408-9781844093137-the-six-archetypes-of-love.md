          Addressing the need to understand the role of love in life, this
          compendium of startling insights relates love to the spiritual
          development in each of six universal archetypes. Attempting to answer
          such questions as What is love? How do we find it and how can we keep
          it? Why are there so many puzzling forms of it? and Why do so many
          people get it so wrong?, the book shows how love relates to the
          self-awareness in spiritual development for each archetype. Whether
          describing an Innocent, a Magician, a Monarch, an Orphan, a Pilgrim,
          or a Warrior-Lover, the featured archetype profiles offer essential
          guidance about what level of awareness is currently being lived, how
          to transition to the next stage, and how love can be nurtured. Each
          stage of development is tied to the ancient wisdom of the Tarot--the
          visual images of which act as reminders as to what to expect on life's
          journey--supported by real-life and pop culture examples that provide
          more immediate accessibility.
