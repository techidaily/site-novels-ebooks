          This revised and updated edition of a classic book on dream

          proven technique to recall and record dreams while they are still
          fresh in the dreamer's mind by grouping similar images, feelings, and
          characters into affinity circles. By pinpointing the feelings and
          images of dreams and identifying them with the dreamer's waking life,
