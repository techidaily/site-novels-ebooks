          <p>
            In Tarology Enrique Enriquez sees the Tarot de Marseille through the
            prism and science of pataphysics, the science of imaginary
            solutions. By following into the footsteps of Oulipian writers, he
            applies the idea of constraint and the rule of restriction to the
            surprisingly visual and gestural nature of Tarot.
          </p>
          <p>
            The result is not only illuminating but also enriching for all those
            interested in the history of Tarot and its divinatory practices.
            Enriquez develops a whole new method of reading cards, which
            combines careful considerations of chance with choice.
          </p>
          <p>
            By using a phenomenological and constructivist approach to the
            cards, Enriquez shows how the Tarot de Marseille speaks poetry and
            thus reveals some of our deepest concerns with language, with what
            we can say when we are at a loss for words.
          </p>
