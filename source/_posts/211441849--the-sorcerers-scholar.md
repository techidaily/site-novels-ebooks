          more than 600 magical prescriptions from both pagan and Christian
          sources from the last 2,000 years. He provides an in-depth look at
          protection magic, including healing spells from rare texts and from
          the heavily guarded gypsy (Rom) tradition. In
          <i>The High Magic of Talismans and Amulets</i>, he provides a
          comprehensive history of the use of talismans and amulets for
          protection, healing, and divine influence. He reviews many different
          kinds of amulets and talismans used throughout the ages, such as a
          rabbit’s foot, horseshoe, gris-gris bag, and inscribed parchment
          charged through ritual, detailing the magical principles behind each
          object and showing that their use is still as widespread today as any
          time in the past.
