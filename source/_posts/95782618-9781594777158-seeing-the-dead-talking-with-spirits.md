          see the spirit of a deceased woman that was being honored there--a
          sign that marked the beginning of a remarkable adventure that taught
          her how to use her gift for healing herself and others. <br />

          Often the pain caused by unresolved psychological traumas from the
          past--including past lives--is the key obstacle restricting access to
          the powers offered by the spirits. The first step in healing requires
          reconnecting with the joy that resides within us. Once this connection
          has been established, we are all capable of restoring our connection
          to the spirit world. Alexandra Leclere’s story provides a roadmap for
          finding our way back.
