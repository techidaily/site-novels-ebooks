          <p>
            Here is a personal story of "a student who sought and became the
            pupil of one deeply learned in the knowledge of the Divine
            Sciences"; a moving account of Juste's journey through the jungle of
            falsehoods and half-truths in his search for Truth. Anarchism,
            atheism, socialism, spiritualism, theosophy--all these and more
            Juste investigates in the hope that they will supply a comprehensive
            philosophy of life, one which can answer the many perplexing
            questions that often vex thinking men and women. Along the way, he
            meets many weird and fantastic characters, culminating in the
            meeting of his own true Teacher, whose rare pearls of wisdom are
            scattered throughout. Included among the many topics discussed are:
            Theosophy, false teachers and teachings, vegetarianism, science,
            magic, art, initiation, health, meditation, and the Astral World.
          </p>
