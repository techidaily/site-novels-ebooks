          Faced with unrelenting stresses from daily news, relationships,
          health, and financial conditions, and unsatisfied with the temporary
          and side-effect-riddled relief that pharmaceuticals provide, millions
          are finding measures of peace and positive energy through mindful
          breathing practices. In this book, Stanislav Grof, Neil Douglas-Klotz,
          Sharon G. Mijares, Sonia Gilbert, Sheldon Kramer, Ilse Middendorf,
          Michael Sky, Puran Bair, and other well-known experts and
          international workshop leaders take up a wide range of Western,
          Eastern, and Middle Eastern breathing practices, describing the
          historical development of these techniques and philosophies, and
          providing examples of modern practices, stories of healing, and
          specific exercises for application.
