          What if our teeth are a reflection of who we are? What if dental

          personal experience, the author states that this approach can even
          remedy ailments such as chronic rhinitis and otitis, eczema,
          allergies, asthma, back problems and sleep disorders.Montaud describes
          his personal journey of discovery, initially to help his suffering
          son. Now, after decades of experience and numerous eloquent clinical
          results, he demonstrates that this human-based approach to our mouth
          and teeth can stimulate, at any age, the extraordinary healing
          potential of our bodies. With case studies and supporting photographic
          evidence, he shows that Dentosophy improves the general health of
          patients, both physical and mental. This illustrated and accessible
          book offers an exciting new perspective on our teeth and their innate
          wisdom.
