          Written to fill the gap in available knowledge on trance, prophecy,
          deity-possession and mediumship within the neo-Pagan and Wiccan
          communities, Lifting the Veil has been developed from Janet Farrar and
          Gavin Bone's personal work and public workshops on trance-prophecy and
          ecstatic ritual over 25 years. The book covers the history and modern
          practice of trance as well as the methods of practice.It also explores
          the four keys to trance-prophecy, which include the importance of
          understanding mythical cosmology and psychology, understanding the
          role of energy in trance, the nature of spirits and deity, and
          understanding what trance is and the techniques involved. Because
          trance-prophecy is a very subjective process, the book includes
          descriptions of the personal experiences of others and transcriptions
          from several independent sessions by modern seers and priestesses.
