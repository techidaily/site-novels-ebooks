          What is a sacred moment? Is it a big, sudden change in the direction
          of your life or your physical or mental state? Or is it a moment in

          We may find our lives full of fear, hurt, or pain of loss, and even

            >Small Steps, Huge Changes: The Extraordinary Moments of an Ordinary
            Life</i
          >, writer Phyllis Reed shows us how it is possible to discover healing
          and joy by choosing to take just one small, courageous step.
          <p>
            Through reflections, remembrances, poems, and vignettes, Reed tracks
            her own small steps through realms as varied as love, parenthood,
            loneliness, fear, and connecting to places, other people, and holy
            presence. Each true story, told in Reeds conversational, nurturing
            tone, is a tribute to those who have found ways to live happily and
            healthilyafter great difficultiesto see the extraordinary in their
            everyday experiences.
          </p>
