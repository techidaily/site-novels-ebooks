          book is based on the Buddha's teachings and the practice of Zen, and

          humorous and lighthearted style, it illustrates through essays,
          stories, and examples what keeps us from choosing well-being, love,
          happiness, and joy as our life experience. In addition, the book

          those who have practiced with these teachings.
