          Rudolf Steiner's inspiring words provide rich and nourishing thoughts

          year. Carefully selected by Jean-Claude Lin, the quotations are
          sourced from lectures and addresses that Steiner gave on the specific

          foundation of love; love is the fruit of wisdom reborn in the I.'

          lecturing activity – to which this book gives manifold entry points.

          omega of the human being who strives for truth and freedom'.
