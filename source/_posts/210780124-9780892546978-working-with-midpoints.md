          <b
            >A simple yet often overlooked astrological technique. The
            information that midpoints provide is astonishing; they can mirror
            what is going on in the world, in many cases providing the missing

            astrology fails.</b
          ><br /><br />
          Veteran astrologer and author Kathy Allan has crafted a new manual
          that will teach readers to hone their basic chart reading and
          predictive skills through working with midpoints, long considered a
          specialized topic in the field of astrology. Initially inspired by the
          astrology classic <i>Working with Astrology</i> by Charles Harvey and
          Mike Harding,&nbsp; Allan wanted to create a more user-friendly
          textbook. Exercises are included at the end of each chapter. After
          explaining what midpoints are and how to find them,&nbsp;Allan uses
          in-depth case studies that examine the lives and horoscopes of such
          cultural icons as Carl Jung, Evangeline Adams, and Stephen King,
          demonstrating midpoints in action. Allan shows us how to delineate
          transiting and solar arc midpoints as an aid to understanding events.
          Adding midpoints to ingress, eclipse, and return charts adds detailed
          information that enables us to more accurately anticipate coming
          events.&nbsp;<br /><br />
          Readers will learn all about occupied and unoccupied midpoints,
          planetary axes, the 360 and 90 degree dials, plus how to contemplate
          and forecast future trends. At last, here is a systematic and engaging
          approach to – working with midpoints.&nbsp;<br /><br />
          The perfect companion volume to Allan’s acclaimed book on the Lunar
          Nodes.<br />
