          <p>
            The kitchen has always been the center of the home. The Goddess
            Vesta was the goddess of the hearth. While we don't cook on hearths
            today, we do have kitchens where we practice our spiritual concepts,
            since the food that we cook, and the herbs and aromatics we use to
            change the consciusness are prepared here--whether we use a stove or
            a fireplace. This is a book about how to permeate your home with
            your work, and will appeal to people interested in wicca, shamanism,
            neo-paganism, or those who practice any religion that deals with
            developing inner strength, love, and healing. Janet Thompson
            discusses medicinal and magical herbs, spells, incense, tokens,
            amulets, working with color, aromatherapy, crystals, purification,
            baths, ritual, moon phases, the pathwork to the crone, the witches
            wheel (the festivals), and recipes. She provides practical insights
            for people who are just starting to live the pagan way!
