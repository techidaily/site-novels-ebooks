          <p>
            This book takes the reader on a journey to discovering the
            life-transforming power of forgiveness. It serves as a guide to
            uncovering the physical, mental, emotional and spiritual benefits
            that can be enjoyed by adopting a spirit of forgiveness. Filled with
            real-life stories demonstrating the power of forgiveness, the book
            explores the How, Why and What of forgiveness, including
            self-forgiveness and accepting forgiveness when offered, and
            addresses key related topics such dealing with anger, the power of
            prayer, and learning to apologize. It includes a list of quotes
            dealing with forgiveness and anger as well as a list of key Biblical
            verses on these subjects. Written by someone who has struggled to be
            forgiving, it is the author's hope and intent that the reader will
            take to heart just how transformative a spirit of forgiveness can be
            in their life and in the lives of the people in their lives.
