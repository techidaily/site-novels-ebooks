          loving and being loved - that will transform the way we live, and the
          way we relate to God, here and now. For example, God's question to

          already possess what we think we're searching for; Moses' desert
          experiences of grief and glory encourage us that we journey forward to
          the Promised Land, while Elijah's powerlessness reminds us that God
          often calls us out of darkness and weakness, for we may need stillness
          to hear him.
