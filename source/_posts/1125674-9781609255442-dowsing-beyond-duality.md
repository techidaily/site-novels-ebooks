            also to deepen one’s self-realization, to move beyond the duality of
            the third dimension, and create positive change in one’s life and in
            the world. With more than 75 charts, the Cowans guide readers
            through specific movements of the pendulum that release limiting
            beliefs and unconscious resistance.
