            meant to flower into intuition. <br /><br />In this volume, Osho

            identify its functioning in others and ourselves. You will learn to
            distinguish between genuine intuitive insight and the “wishful
            thinking” that can often lead to mistaken choices and unwanted
            consequences. Includes many specific exercises and meditations
