            contemporary times, Entering Hekate’s Garden merges historical

            especially its application to the Green World. The book features

            lavender.

            importance of layering, using botanicals in spells, rituals, altars,
            and more, as well as ways to develop meaningful relationships with
            the pharmakoi (master plant spirits). Poetry, petitions, and musings
            about pharmakeia are woven throughout.
