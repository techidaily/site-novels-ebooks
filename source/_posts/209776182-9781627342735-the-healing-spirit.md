          <i>The Healing Spirit</i> explores the idea that there is healing in
          dialogue. Using examples from Judaic, Christian and Buddhist
          philosophies and drawing on his personal and professional experiences,
          Dr. Perri explains how he believes the healing spirit emerges when two
          people unconditionally encounter each other. Introducing his concepts
          of the invisible matrix, archetypal language, and the naked essentials
          of relation he draws on the works of Martin Buber, Carl G. Jung and
          Abraham Joshua Heschel and their germinal contribution to the topic.
          <i>The Healing Spirit</i> can be a valuable guide to anyone in the
          healing or helping professions or anyone who simply wishes to help
          another. <i>The Healing Spirit</i> explores many of the personal
          encounters of people in the depths of sorrow, and how meaningful
          interpersonal dialogue generated the essence of spirit in their lives.
          Dr. Perri embraces the idea that spirit emerges both within and
          between us when spirit is beckoned. It is the spirit that heals. It is
          <i>The Healing Spirit.</i>
