          <i>The Tarot of the Spirit</i>&nbsp;is a companion book to the tarot
          deck painted by Joyce Eakins and is meant to pick up where other
          tarots leave off.&nbsp; It is here to aid serious students on the
          esoteric path of the Western Mysteries.&nbsp; Its reason for being is
          to provide assistance for inner awakening and continued spiritual
          practice.&nbsp;Centered on the Qabbalistic Tree of Life, this
          symbolism clearly explores the Minor Arcana as a representation of the
          four components of life: spirit, emotion, intellect, and body while it
          reveals the Major Arcana to be the keys to our emotional response
          patterns to the symbolic universe in which we live. Includes seven
          monthly meditations, individual readings, and layouts.&nbsp;Every
          concept presented in the book is essential in its context. Nothing has
          been reduced, neither has it been convoluted.&nbsp; Every effort has
          been made to keep the interpretations clearly understandable.&nbsp;
          Both this book and the deck are meant to be referred to again and
          again.&nbsp; As the knowledge of the student deepens, the texts will
          take on greater meaning.
