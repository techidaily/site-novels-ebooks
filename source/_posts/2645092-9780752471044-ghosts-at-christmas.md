          Featuring eyewitness accounts, a unique history of how Christmas came

          From Charles Dickens' famous A Christmas Carol to Tim Burton's The
          Nightmare Before Christmas, the festive season has long been closely
          associated with ghostly tales. This fascinating work looks not only at
          the fictional treatment of Christmas ghosts but also at a host of

          contemporary sources and illustrated with over 70 images, this

          measure and is guaranteed to spice up the festive season.
