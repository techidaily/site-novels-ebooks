          <p>
            Michael Janes was introduced to Buddhism in the midst of his
            recovery from drug addiction. Realizing that the selfishness he grew
            accustomed to no longer applied Michael sought to change. A vast
            collection of personal notes became his inspiration to write a book.
          </p>
          <p>&nbsp;</p>
          <p>
            Innermore is a snapshot of the roughly one thousand pages of
            Michael's writings that he compiled over his nearly twenty years of
            spiritual practice. It follows his quest to try and understand
            people, society, and ultimately himself.
          </p>
          <p>&nbsp;</p>
          <p>
            Michael was searching for something. He was longing for a sense of
            connection, but this connection was not easy for him. Instead of
            growing discouraged Michael decided to express himself through words
            on paper. Using poetry, imagery and plenty of imagination he ended
            up painting a picture of his world as he was living it. The result
            was unexpected. Michael found the connection he was looking for, and
            he transformed his life.
          </p>
          <p>&nbsp;</p>

            You're invited to come explore Michael's journey in this
            one-of-a-kind book! It is a unique telling of the Buddhist
            experience playing out real time in one man's life. It transformed
            Michael, and it just may do the same for you. &nbsp;
