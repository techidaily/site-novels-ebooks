          Psychedelic Research, Imperial College London, and Lecturer on the MSc
          Consciousness, Spirituality and Transpersonal Psychology for Alef
          Trust and Liverpool John Moores University.
        </p>

          He was President of the Para-psychological Association, founded the
          Ecology, Cosmos and Consciousness salon in London and is a cofounder
          and director of Breaking Convention: International Conference on
          Psychedelic Consciousness. He has studied techniques of consciousness
          alteration from South America to India, from the perspectives of
          science and shamanism.
