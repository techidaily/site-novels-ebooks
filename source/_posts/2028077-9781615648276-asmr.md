          ASMR stands for Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response. A way to beat
          insomnia and reduce stress, ASMR is a relaxed, tingling feeling that
          many people report having as a response to particular stimuli, such as
          close personal attention, tapping, or whispering.
          <i>Idiot's Guides: ASMR</i> offers a clear explanation of its
          benefits, different trigger types, and how to experience its effects.
          Bonus content includes exclusive interviews with the top ASMR artists
          and online video content.
