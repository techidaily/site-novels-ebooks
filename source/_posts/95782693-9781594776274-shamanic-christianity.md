          A guide to reconnecting with Jesus, Mary, and the saints as shamanic
          teachers of divine mysteries<br /><br />• Contains meditations,
          contemplations, parables, and active ritual tasks that help bring
          forth a shamanic understanding and practice of Christianity<br /><br />•
          Shows shamanic experience to be the root of mystical communion<br /><br />When
          the missionaries came to North America to “save” the American Indians,
          they were perplexed to discover that while they talked
          <i>about</i> Jesus, some of the Indians claimed to talk directly
          <i>with</i> him. Among Christians there is almost complete silence on
          the subject of the place of shamanism in experiencing the divine, yet
          shamanic experience is at the root of all mystical communion.
          <br /><br /><i>Shamanic Christianity</i> offers a chance to rekindle
          the shamanic practices of Christianity to those who wish to restore
          their direct connection to the spirit world. In the tradition of
          contemplative practice, this reconnection takes the form of devotions.
          Presented in four forms, these devotions begin with a specific
          contemplation, followed by a meditative focus, then a parable from the
          author’s own visionary experiences, and finally an active mystical
          practice to help ground the meditations and contemplations in a ritual
          or ceremony that involves active participation. These four forms serve
          to reintroduce Jesus, Mary, and the historically renowned saints as
          shamanic teachers of divine mysteries whose spiritual presence is
          readily available to contemporary lives. The author also presents
          specific directives for handling everyday challenges in a
          shamanic-inspired manner, drawing upon creative activities and
          resources that encourage approaching the world with the imaginative
          and playful spirit of a child, whose personal freedom and creative
          expression is always wide open to possibilities.
