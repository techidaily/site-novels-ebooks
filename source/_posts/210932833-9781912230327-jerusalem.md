          the Word, which could be actualized on earth – in the human being.In
          Jerusalem Dr Ben-Aharon describes the evolution of the Hebrew people
          and its role in the development of the human race. The journey
          continues to the present day, where the universal human Self has the
          potential to become a free participator in the ongoing creation of the
          universe.
