            This book is my story of how God brought good out of a very bad

            has a purpose and plan for my life.He showed me a different path
            than I had ever traveled before. Once I realized how to be still,
            listen to God, and allow the Holy Spirit to work in my life, I found
            my purpose in writing the messages He gives me, and I enjoy sharing
            them to encourage others and lead them to Christ. He used a horrible
            year to change my life forever. I felt compelled to write this book
            not only for my own healing, but if even one person who reads it is
            touched, inspired, or encouraged in some way, it is worth it. I find
            it very ironic that God has me revealing the things I hid for so
            long. My life is not my own. I was purchased with His blood. I
            believe it is my duty to respect my body, guard my heart, and put on
            the whole armor of God every day as I surrender myself to Him in
            every way. As a faithful servant of Christ, I am humbled and
