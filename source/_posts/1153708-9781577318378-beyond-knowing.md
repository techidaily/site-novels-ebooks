        <div accordion-content="" id="description">
          Working as a medical examiner, Dr. Janis Amatuzio has found that by
          listening and talking to loved ones of the deceased, she can offer
          them a sense of closure. In doing so, she has heard — and here retells
          — extraordinary stories of spiritual and otherworldly events
          surrounding the transition between life and death.As in her first
          book, Forever Ours, Dr. Amatuzio presents the amazing, heartfelt
          accounts told to her by grieving family members, patients, doctors,
          nurses, clergy, and police officers. Along with these stories, she
          shares her own story — reflecting on the course of her career, the
          bonds she has formed over the years, the lessons she has learned, and
          her conclusion that “Everything truly is all right.”This powerful book
          honors the mystery of life and death, exploring the realms of visions,
          synchronicities, and communications on death’s threshold. Told in the
          voice of a compassionate scientist who sees death every day, these
          stories eloquently convey the patterns of truth Dr. Amatuzio has found
          in what she sees and hears. Beyond Knowing explores the wisdom the
          living might find in these accounts and shows how that wisdom changes
          lives.
