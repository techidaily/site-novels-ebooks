          In 1945, the Japanese cities of Hiroshima and Nagasaki became the
          first and last victims of the atom bomb, the most destructive man-made

          civilizations cracked the secrets of the atom, only to become the
          victims of its awesome, terrifying power. Still others may have been
          destroyed by hostile aliens with their own nuclear arsenals.<br /><br />Where
          is the evidence? The answer is shockingly simple: it’s everywhere.

          cities of Sodom and Gomorrah and the ancient Pakistani culture of
          Mohenjo-daro, Pakistan, to the Lonar Crater in India and the
          revelations in the Ramayana and Mahabharata, two ancient Sanskrit
          texts that describe nuclear warfare thousands of years ago.
