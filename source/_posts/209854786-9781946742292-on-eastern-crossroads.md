            direction. But readers may be enriched in their intuition regarding
            the constructive aspects of the story of mankind.Thus we see how the
            minds of the nations collected and preserved forages the great
            images as evolving sparks of their genius.<br />These sparks should
            be guarded with equal solicitude by ourselvesand our posterity. It
            is chiefly necessary that we should regard with reverence these
            traditions, nor demean them, but let us apply them to our spiritual
            necessities in the most constructive and most appreciative way.<br />Thus,
