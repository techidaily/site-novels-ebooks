            The ability to see into the future and to accurately predict events

            riveting new account, the predictions of the most famous sages and

            prophecies and predictions form the core of this book.<br /><br />Illustrated

            role of astrology, alchemy and arcane knowledge in prophecy. Most
            intriguingly, it tries to answer that age-old question of whether
            there is such a thing as 'secret knowledge' and whether it is
            possible for mere mortals to access it.
