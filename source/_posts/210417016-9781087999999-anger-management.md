          <p>
            Anger and rage are known syndromes and as societies face the
            explosion of reckless emotional discharges, the casualties will
            continue to increase. There is a need for emotional healing today.
            Societal breakdowns are signs of accumulated problems - stemming
            from inside wounds and hurts that people are incubating or carrying
            around in their lives. The results are sudden outbursts of anger and
            rage, often placing the lives of other people in harm's way. The
            world is not in the position to vet every person's emotional
            stability, which is to say that many provisions we have, are
            relatively insufficient.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <p><br /></p>
          <p>
            Anger management is a community affair that requires careful
            investigation into the fabrics of people's lives on a continuous
            basis. Crops tend to grow better and bring forth increased fruits
            when they are cared for. Every harvest is a result of extensive
            investment and building up people's lives is a more delicate work.
            To manage anger, we will need all the tools or resources we can
            gather - from all departments including governments, corporate
            entities, families and individuals. This is a battle that we must
            all join together to fight to the finish! Everyone must bring what
            is necessary to the table! Anger management is a recipe - a manual
            textbook of practical recommendations and diagnostic tools to help
            you navigate through the dangerous terrains of uncontrollable anger
            and rage.
