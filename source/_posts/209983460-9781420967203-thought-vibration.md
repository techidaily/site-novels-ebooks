          positive outcomes. First published in 1906, Atkinson’s work remains a

          changing the direction of one’s life. Full of practical advice and
          helpful exercises to assist the reader into putting these theories
          into action, readers will be inspired to apply the power of positive
          thinking in their daily lives. “Thought Vibration” remains as relevant
          and encouraging as when it was first published over a century ago.
