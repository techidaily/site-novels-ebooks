        <div accordion-content="" id="description">
          The art of divining, or dowsing, has for years been cloaked in mystery
          and superstition. Recognized by some as a means of finding water and
          minerals, it is actually a method for anyone to develop intuitive
          skills. This thought-provoking book demystifies dowsing and presents
          this ancient science as a powerful tool for self-knowledge. <br />
          <br />Includes a brief history of dowsing, and step-by-step
          instructions for learning to use L-rods, Y-rods, and pendulums, as
          well as non-device dowsing. Shows how dowsing is used for locating
          water, gold and other minerals, buried artifacts, ancient ritual
          sites, and in police work to help find missing persons.
