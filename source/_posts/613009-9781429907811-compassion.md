            perspective, Osho reveals that “passion” lies at the root of the

            others but to force them to change. <br /><br />Using stories from
            the lives of Jesus and Buddha and the world of Zen, Osho shows how
            the path to authentic compassion arises from within, beginning with
            a deep acceptance and love of oneself. Only then, says Osho, does
            compassion flower into a healing force, rooted in the unconditional
            acceptance of the other as he or she is.<br /><br /><b
              >Osho challenges readers to examine and break free of the
