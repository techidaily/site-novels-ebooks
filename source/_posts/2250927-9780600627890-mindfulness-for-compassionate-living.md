          <p>
            Self-compassion is a life-changing way of thinking that is rooted in
            Mindfulness. By learning to have more loving kindness and
            forgiveness for ourselves, we have more empathy and compassion for
            others. <br /><br />The self-compassion movement has been growing
            rapidly over the past two years with recent research into the
            neuroscience of compassion showing that changing our thinking habits
            makes new neural pathways in the brain - and that compassion can be
            learned. <br /><br />The benefits of this practice are personal and
            global, from learning to manage chronic pain to relieving stress,
            boosting the immune system and circulation, along with improving
            relationships as we connect more authentically with others.
