          to nature must be fostered. Weaving together Morris’s personal
          witchcraft gnosis, <i>Celtic Hedge Witchery&nbsp;</i>focuses on

          spaces. <i>Celtic Hedge Witchery&nbsp;</i>offers a revolutionary take
          on what it means to be a hedgewitch in the modern era and empowers the
