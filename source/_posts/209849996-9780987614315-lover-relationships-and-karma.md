            Intimate passionate love has been the undying quest of human beings

            arts. The desire to be intimately related to another to whom one

            the deepest psycho-spiritual karmas that drive the meeting. It
            provides signposts that herald danger and strategies for maximising
            the potential strength in your intimate relationship.
