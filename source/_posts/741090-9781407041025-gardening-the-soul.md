          <p>
            Sister Stan, as she is affectionately known, was brought up on a
            farm in Dingle, County Kerry, one of the most beautiful parts of
            Ireland. It was there that she learnt to appreciate the earth, its
            stillness and its energy, its beauty and its bounty.<br /><br />In
            this hugely powerful and evocative book, Sister Stan looks to the
            earth that is so precious to our existence for inspiration
            throughout the year. Reflecting the garden's changing rhythms
            through the seasons, <i>Gardening the Soul</i> offers us a daily
            thought to keep us going as we face the challenges of modern
            life.<br /><br />All our moods are covered here... <br />* in
            January, when there is silence in the garden, she looks at Solitude
            in our soul...<br />* in March, with emergence in the garden, she
            offers Hope...<br />* in August, when there is fullness and
            abundance everywhere, there is Blessing, and<br />* in October, the
            time of harvest, there is Harmony <br /><br />Comforting and
            insightful, <i>Gardening the Soul</i> is an inspirational daybook of
            lessons gleaned from the wisdom of nature.
