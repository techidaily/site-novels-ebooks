            of enlarging our emotional, mental, and physical relationship with
            the divine reality that is present in us, but one not ordinarily
            accessible to our emotions or concepts. The spiritual journey
            teaches us: firstly, to believe in the Divine Indwelling within us,
            fully present and energizing every level of our being; secondly, to
            recognize that this energy is benign, healing, and transforming; and
            thirdly, to enjoy its gradual unfolding step-by-step both in prayer
            and action.
