          <p>
            Quantum physics, black holes, astronomy… we often ponder and
            deliberate these topics across all social stratums, our workplaces,
            places of worship, and on social media. We are confident that a
            discussion of these subjects will not produce a round of derision
            from our peers. But what if we asked our co-workers about their
            encounters with otherworldly beings? What if we asked our friends if
            they have ever had contact with an alien?
          </p>
          <p>
            In Bad Mind, Karen Cavalli explores the possibility of
            extraterrestrial beings and what that means for us in this world. As
            she details examples from her life and others she has encountered,
            she lays out for the reader what it means to have an encounter with
            spirit and how it can guide us to a deeper understanding of our
            reality.
          </p>
