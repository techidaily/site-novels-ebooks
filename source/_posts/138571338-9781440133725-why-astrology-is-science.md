          In todays world, at least 90 percent of Americans under age thirty are
          said to know their Sun sign, yet no one has ever really delved into
          answering the question: Is astrology really a science?
          <p>
            Tapan Das earned master and doctorate degrees in electronic
            engineering and his passion for astrology is evident as he shares
            the results of his years of intense research into the fascinating
            and complex realm of astrology and cosmology. Das clarifies why
            astrology is based on statistical analysis, provides reasons why
            astrology is a science, and explains each reason in detail. A
            concise overview of astrology is included, providing an introduction
            describing why astrology can predict how the stars and planets will
            influence a persons life, positively or negatively, and explaining
            both the past and present as well as outline the future.
          </p>
          <p>
            <i>Why Astrology is Science: Five Good Reasons</i> is written for
            scientists, astrologers, and those with a keen interest to learn a
            new perspective on astrology that is sure to inspire a renewed sense
            of wonder about stars and planets and how their patterns and
            relationships hold significant meaning for all of us.
