          <p>
            What was it like to be a member of the well-known
            <strong>Findhorn community </strong>in Scotland? In this memoir,
            Bonnie Blue describes her <strong>psychological</strong> and
            <strong>spiritual</strong> journey whilst there. In the intense
            crucible of community life, she details the lessons she learned,
            relevant for anyone on their own spiritual journey. Each chapter is
            a step forward in her becoming a more <strong>conscious</strong> and
            <strong>empowered</strong> woman. Her experiences take her through
            times of <strong>healing</strong> and
            <strong>awakenings</strong> that later inform her work as a
            <strong>psychotherapist</strong>. All of this she shares freely, her
            writing intensely personal and self-reflective.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <p>
            Considered by many to be a mystery school of the twentieth century,
            we learn about the day-to-day life at Findhorn. This memoir can be
            seen as a primer for learning about and applying techniques commonly
            used in the community. Examples of <strong>inner guidance</strong>,
            <strong>manifestation</strong>, and
            <strong>cooperation with the nature kingdom</strong> weave through
            the author's vividly detailed journey to a more
            <strong>soul-infused life</strong>. Her <strong>dreams</strong> and
            the symbolism found in the everyday are linked to her transformative
            path. Many of her stories may seem fantastical, yet what is conveyed
            is the magic that is possible in a normal person's life if one only
            looks for it.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <p>
            Each chapter ends with thoughtful commentary and suggests useful
            practices. She makes it clear anyone can create a more enriching
            relationship with their own <strong>soul</strong>, by looking
            within.&nbsp;
          </p>
