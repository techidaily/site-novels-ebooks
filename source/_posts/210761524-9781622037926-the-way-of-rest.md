            <i>The Way of Rest</i> gathers nearly 200 of Jeff Foster’s most

            depression—Jeff invites us to contemplate “the Way of Rest” and its

            Discomfort and discontent—how our frustrations become an opening to
            deep peace<br />• Love—rediscovering who we are beyond our carefully
            constructed facades<br />• Silence—discovering the vibrancy of
            living by letting go of thoughts and concepts<br />“I hope the words
            in this book inspire, challenge, and encourage you. But most of all,
            I hope they help you remember that you are life, inseparable from
            the power that grows the flowers and gives birth to galaxies,”
            writes Jeff Foster. <i>The Way of Rest </i>brings you his
