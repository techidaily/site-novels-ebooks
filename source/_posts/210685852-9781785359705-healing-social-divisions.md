          We live at a time when societies are riven with division and strife.
          What hope is there for us to heal? Healing Social
          Divisions&nbsp;provides a radically new, non-ideological and effective
          consciousness- based approach for transforming our societies.
          Peer-reviewed research provides evidence that it is possible to
          neutralise stress in the collective consciousness of a society. This
          then promotes life, liberty, happiness, heals social divisions and
          creates the platform for good governance. The research challenges the
          conventional wisdom that consciousness is only a by-product of brain
          functioning. Along the route it examines our self-concept, freeing us
          from the biases of reductionism that impede the development of
          morality in our public life.
