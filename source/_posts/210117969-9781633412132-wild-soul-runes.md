          <p>
            Using inquiry and original translations alongside traditional rune
            poems and sacred art, <i>Wild Soul Runes</i> invites readers to
            develop their own wisdom connection with the runes through practice.
            The book is based on the premise that all of us possess the ability
            to receive divine information through rune practice. Rather than
            telling the reader what the runes mean, author Lara Veleda Vesta
            shares an interactive practice for readers to discover the unique
            ways the runes speak to them.
          </p>
          <p>
            The book covers the history of the runes, both in myth and through
            the connection with Old European archaeological findings. This
            history offers evidence for the idea that the runes have a feminine
            origin, coming from the well of the Norns, the female triple
            giantesses who represent the fates in Norse myth.
          </p>
          <p>
            It introduces the concept of the runes as beings rather than simply
            an alphabet or magical tools. The purpose of Wild Soul Runes is to
            create a relationship with these beings through ancestral
            connection, personal gnosis, and ritual practice.
          </p>
