            &nbsp;&nbsp;This manuscript is a compilation of ten years of

            couldn't remember. The dream was about my wife, Mary Jo, who had
            died eleven years prior. All I remembered was, "What if I told

            Judaism, Shinto, Sikhism, Taoism, and Zoroastrianism. There are
            fundamental lines of truth in all belief systems. The Book of Truths
            is for All. This manuscript is dedicated to Mary Jo Hall. -Bob
