        <p>
          Johnny Moses is a Tulalip Native American raised in the remote
          Nuu-chah-nulth village of Ohiat on the west coast of Vancouver Island.
          He was raised in the traditional ways and sent by his elders to share
          their teachings with all people. Johnny is a master-storyteller, oral
          historian, traditional healer and respected spiritual leader. Fluent
          in nine Native languages, and carrier of the Si.Si.Wiss medicine
          teachings, Johnny Moses shares the richness of Pacific Northwest
          Coastal cultural traditions. He currently resides in Boulder Creek,
          CA.
