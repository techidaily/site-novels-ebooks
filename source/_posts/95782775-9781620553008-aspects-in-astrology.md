          Faculty of Astrological Studies in London.<br /><br />Aspects are an
          essential piece of the astrological puzzle when it comes to
          interpreting and using the information that astrology can give us. It
          is not enough to know the placement of the planets on the horoscope.
          It is the <i>relationships</i> between the stars and planets that let
          us grasp the destiny of the individual. Aspects describe the drama of
          our lives--the complex configurations that influence what will happen
          to us over time. <br /><br />Sue Tompkins, a fellow of the Faculty of
          Astrological Studies in London, shows how to interpret aspects when
          doing daily astrological readings. She uses the lives of real people
          to plot the aspects and offers examples of every possible planetary
          combination. Detailing the influence of oppositions, trines and
          elemental trines, and cardinal, mutable, and fixed crosses, Tompkins
          shows how aspects provide the energy in the chart that transforms the
          horoscope into something symbolizing an alive and vital human being.
          With <i>Aspects in Astrology</i>, Tompkins provides both the novice
          and the experienced astrologer the evidence and concrete methods
          needed to grasp the vast knowledge offered to us by our horoscope.
