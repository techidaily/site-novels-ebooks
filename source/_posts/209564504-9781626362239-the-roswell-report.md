          U.S. Air Force completed a thousand-page report to explain the events
          that transpired in and near Roswell in the summer of 1947. That report
          sought to bring all the facts to light, declassify all the documents,
          and present the definitive truth to the public.
          <i>The Roswell Report: Case Closed </i>is the follow up to that report

          complete, open, and final explanation of the events that occurred in
          the Southwest many years ago. While this explanation may not be as
