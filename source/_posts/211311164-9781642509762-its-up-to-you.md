            insidious ways we create misery or drama, try to impose will, and
            suffer needlessly, and as we follow her gentle prompts to make
            different choices, we begin to see that we can change just about
            anything in our lives by taking these small steps. As Karen Casey
            puts it, “Progress is guaranteed. Perfection isn’t expected.”
