          Creative Living was born of the author’s desire to stay balanced and
          at peace at all times. She began writing this book in the hope of

          respond positively to challenges and navigate justly on the complex
          path of life. On this journey of labor and love, she discovered

          enable you to improve your situation with positive outlooks and
          creative means. You will uplift your vision, have satisfying
