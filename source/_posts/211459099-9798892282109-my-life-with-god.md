          <p>
            I am 85+ years old and this is my first attempt to write a book, in
            this case an autobiography. I was encouraged to endeavor this
            accumulation of personal stories because a dear friend encouraged me
            and said more than once "You have so many great stories." My stories
            relate to my "God moments", my wife Luci of 66+ years, children and
            grandchildren, my education, my Navy career, including Viet Nam, my
            retirement years as an ordained Deacon in the Catholic church and
            presently as a leader and Commander of Post 1 American Legion, in
            Honolulu, Hawaii. My hope is that someone will be touched by my
            story and will heal because of it.
