          Mark Anthony Lord, Pastor of the Unity Naples Church, invites us to
          consider a new commandment for the 21st century:<br /><br />
          Thou shall not suffer!<br /><br />
          In Thou Shall Not Suffer: Seven Steps to a Life of Joy, author Lord
          shows the reader that it’s not the circumstances of our lives that
          causes our suffering, but instead it’s how we react to those
          circumstances that causes us discomfort. Throughout the book, Lord
          invites us revisit our ideas about God and spirituality, and in the
          process, enter a world of joy, harmony, and abundance.<br /><br />
          With passion and clarity, Lord leads the reader away from old patterns
          of guilt, remorse, and self-judgment, and into a new era of joy and
          peace.&nbsp; Lord’s seven-step model includes lessons on the
          importance of forgiveness, surrender, and self acceptance as the
          cornerstones to living in a continual state of gratitude and joy.<br /><br />
          Each of the seven steps contain exercises designed to guide the reader
          to a new way of looking and being in the world. Pastor Lord’s personal
          story is interwoven throughout, and he shows the reader that be
          practicing the seven steps, they will be able to leave suffering and
          pain behind forever.
