          the contents of the School of Spiritual Science; and the work of
          'Vulcan beings' and other planetary entities. Thoresen offers
          reflections on his travels to western England (with its connections to
          Troy) and southern Spain (with its legacy of Moorish occupation). His
          intention is not to create new dogmas or beliefs, but to testify to
          the living reality of metaphysical dimensions of reality – and
          humanity's latent ability to access them.
