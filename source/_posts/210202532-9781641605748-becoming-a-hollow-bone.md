          From the <i>New York Times</i> bestselling author Anne Wilson Schaef
          comes a spiritual guide and everyday living manual that vitally
          respects and integrates the critical lessons to be garnered from
          Native elders from around the globe. In
          <i>Becoming a Hollow Bone</i> Schaef imparts the richness revealed to
          her over many years by elder Native Americans, Aboriginal Australians,
          Maoris, and other Indigenous cultures. Schaef often heard elders from
          an all-embracing variety of Native peoples say, "Our legends tell us
          that a time will come when our wisdom and way of living will be
          necessary to save the planet, and that time is now." Anyone ready to
          move from feeling separate to a profound sense of connectedness, from
          the personal to the global, will find a provocative path in this
          mind-expanding, stunningly spiritual, yet practical book.
