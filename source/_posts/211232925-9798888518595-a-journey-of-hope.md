            The silence was shattered by a deep, thunderous man's voice. It

            one. You are going to do something extraordinary with your life."

          <p>
            After hearing this voice, Bridget suffered a series of life-altering
            events that left her unable to perform simple, rote tasks, caused
            her to avoid all contact with everyone but her immediate family, and
            led to her being admitted to a psychiatric unit for two nights.
            Follow along to see how she embarks on her journey of healing, from
            seeing a psychiatrist and a therapist to finding the right balance
            of medication needed to get her back to where she was before hearing
            the voice.
          </p>

            This book explains what bipolar disorder is, outlines medications
            that work to combat it, describes Bridget's journey with God, and
            talks about how the stigma of mental illness has been somewhat
            alleviated but that there is still work to be done.
          </p>

            Bridget hopes the book brings comfort to people with mental illness
