          <p>
            Everyone has a purpose. And, according to Oprah Winfrey, “Your real
            job in life is to figure out as soon as possible what that is, who

            one of significance. The book’s ten chapters are organized to help
            you recognize the important milestones along the road to
            self-discovery, laying out what you really need in order to achieve
            personal contentment, and what life’s detours are there to teach us.

            and the personal stories that helped set the course for her best
            life. She then brings together wisdom and insights from luminaries
            in a wide array of fields, inspiring readers to consider what
            they’re meant to do in the world and how to pursue it with passion
            and focus. Renowned figures such as Eckhart Tolle, Brene Brown,
            Lin-Manuel Miranda, Elizabeth Gilbert, Jay-Z, and Ellen DeGeneres
            share the greatest lessons from their own journeys toward a life
            filled with purpose.<br /><br />Paired with over 100 awe-inspiring
            photographs to help illuminate the wisdom of these messages,
            <i>The Path Made Clear</i> provides readers with a beautiful
            resource for achieving a life lived in service of your calling –
