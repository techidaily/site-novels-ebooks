            artist this world has seen. This book includes numerous examples of
            the breathtaking likenesses he was able to draw, as those who had

            it comes to convincing someone of survival beyond death, it is the

            portraits, displayed side by side with comparative photos supplied
            by overjoyed relatives after the artist had completed a portrait of
            a loved one whose face they never expected to see again. Read and

            certainty that there is no such thing as death.
