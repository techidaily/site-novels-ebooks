          <span>About the Author</span>
        </h4>
        <p>
          <b>Charles Webster Leadbeater</b>&nbsp;(1847-1934) was a Church of
          England clergyman until he joined the Theosophical Society in 1883. He
          spent some years in Sri Lanka working for the revival of Buddhism, and
          later became the Presiding Bishop of the Liberal Catholic Church,
          which seeks to combine the preservation of the Catholic sacraments
          with the widest measure of freedom of thought and interpretation of
          the scriptures. He was a highly developed clairvoyant and the author
          of over thirty books on the spiritual life and on the psychic nature
          of man. He unfolded and perfected his own psychic faculties under the
          guidance of his Adept teacher and in 1893 began his clairvoyant
          investigations, on occasion collaborating with Annie Besant, the
          second President of the Theosophical Society. His worldwide lectures
          presented a new viewpoint to thousands of people.
        </p>
