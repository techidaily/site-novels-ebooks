          specific area of human activity: Venus shadows speak of tainted love,
          while Mercury shadows reveal challenges of the mind. Providing vivid
          examples, the author details the meaning of each shadow counterpart
          for every astrological sign and planet that appears in a traditional
          astrological chart and explains how to interpret and work with their
          challenges.<br /><br />In addition to chart interpretation, the author
          explores the history of this astrological method, including its roots
          in ancient Egypt, ancient Greece, and the Western magic tradition. She
          looks at the alchemical nature and subconscious influences of the dark
          side of the human mind and the shadow-work methods necessary for
          bringing the oppositional self into awareness for personal growth and
          conscious evolution.
