          <p>
            Chopping Down the Tree of Knowledge is a work about failing
            -&nbsp;&nbsp;when every road taken should have led to happiness and
            success. It is a look into who is responsible for those failures and
            where God fits into them. Furthermore, it is an investigation into
            the very system we use to make every decision in our lives - our
            belief system.
          </p>
          <p>
            What happens when those systems fail us? Why did they fail? How do
            we change the seemingly negative outcomes, even when we do
            everything according to what we believe to be the right choices?
            Failing is a part of life, but continuing failures and chasing
            dreams that never come to fruition is not. The answers as to why
            successes, and often happiness, seem so elusive is where the journey
            of this written stream-of- consciousness takes us.
          </p>
          <p>
            The author uses personal experiences, scriptures and a leap into the
            shadows within his soul where most won't venture to answer why we
            fail and what happens when we accept why he - and so many others -
            followed the correct paths, and yet wound up so profoundly lost.
            It's a personal work that will touch us in a way which prompts us to
            delve into the depths of our souls and ask if ours and others'
            failures are because we are truly not the people we should be.
          </p>
