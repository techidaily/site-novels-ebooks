            There is a wealth of jargon in the witchcraft community – from words

            book might be called a book of shadows! As more and more people
            enter the witchcraft world, the need for accessible resources to
            de-mystify the most mysterious of practices has never been clearer,
            and The Witch-ionary is the perfect book for baby and beginner
            witches to have at hand.<br /><br />Along the way there are ten
            simple ideas for rituals, spells, and practices to try and put the
            newfound knowledge from within the pages to good use, such as a
            simple house blessing for a happy home, a recipe for anti-anxiety
            bath salts, and the tips and tricks necessary for casting a magick
            circle.<br /><br />Written by Deb Robinson, founder of the hugely

            witch! The perfect book to dip into for an answer to a witchy
            question, The Witch-ionary is the definitive handbook and that all
