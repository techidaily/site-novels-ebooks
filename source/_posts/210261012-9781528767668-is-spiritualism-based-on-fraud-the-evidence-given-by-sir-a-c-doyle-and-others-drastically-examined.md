          Spiritualism is a religious movement based on the belief that spirits

          popularity by the 1920s, particularly in English-speaking countries.

          investigation into the authenticity of spiritualism and related claims
          by Joseph McCabe, examining evidence from many sources and accounts
          including Sir A. C. Doyle. Contents include: “Mediums: Black, White,
