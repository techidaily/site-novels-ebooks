          “By putting survival in a preeminent position, Darwin relegates high
          aspirations and elevated sentiments to the periphery, barely part of
          the landscape, while the utilitarian aspects of life are brought to
          the fore. This vision has had wide repercussions in an age where there
          is an unfortunate tendency to incline towards materialist and
          utilitarian philosophies. These have diffused a primordial and
          decisive feature in man: his spiritual evolution.” “In this work I
          have summarized the theories that I consider the most relevant and
          deserving of analysis and commentary on this subject both from the
          East and the West. I have no doubt that the reader’s spirit will soar
          following the elevated flight of these thinkers.” “In the final
          analysis this is the purpose of all my work: raising the spirit,
          lifting it out of the daily tedium and the noxious and low goals that
          contemporary society has to offer, presenting other realities beyond
          oneself that are more realistic, attractive and worthy of being lived
          when they are shown in the correct light. This work demonstrates other
          ways of seeing, nuances that open multiple windows for the human
          spirit into distant, bright and promising horizons.”
