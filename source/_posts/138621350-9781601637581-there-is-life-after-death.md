          Is death the end? Or, to put it another way, do we survive bodily
          death? Some shrug their shoulders and declare we simply can’t know.
          Others just say “No.” And a few, flying their philosophical colors,
          pretentiously profess to not even understand the question. Curiously,
          the overwhelming majority of human beings throughout the course of
          history have taken it for granted that death is not the end, that
          there is a life after death. This striking and seemingly instinctive
          belief has been embodied in the religious traditions and philosophical
          reflections of most cultures. <i>There is Life After Death</i> is the
          first of its kind in that it assembles and analyzes a comprehensive
          range of data on life after death and then provides a framework to
          understand the data. No previous book has given a concrete structure
          of the afterlife that is based on the accounts of “eye”-witnesses, as
          well as on data from diverse sources. Above all, the book provides
          exciting and compelling answers to the urgent question: what lies on
          the other side?
