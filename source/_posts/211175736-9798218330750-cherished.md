            I believe we all desire to be deeply loved and cherished, even

            unappreciated.

            grocery store parking lot, and ended atop Colorado's red mountain
            soil, reading heartfelt, handwritten vows aloud with a man who is
            without a doubt my soulmate.
          </p>

            decision to do the deep work of healing, we change our energy, and
            things begin to happen for us we never thought possible. It's not
            about finding the right partner. It's about finding our true self
            underneath all the stories, imprints, and stuff that this life has
            us buried under.&nbsp;
