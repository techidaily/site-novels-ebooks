          <p>
            Muddy Mysticism&nbsp;is a spiritual memoir, a lyrical articulation
            of an emergent feminist mysticism and a heartfelt response to the
            lack of mystical literature by women who have chosen a life of
            family, love, work and the world. Like many women she found the
            faith of her childhood no longer fitted... yet still there is a
            longing for the sacred. Through poetry, reflection and experience
            she moves into the possibility of direct experience with the
            divine...beyond a belief system. Exploring the possibility of daily
            life in the modern world not as something to be transcended or
            escaped...but as a mystical path in its own right.
