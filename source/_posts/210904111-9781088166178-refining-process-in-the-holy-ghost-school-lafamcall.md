          <p>
            Refinement, what does it mean? This means, when substances like
            sugar or oil is refined; it is being made pure by the removal of
            other substances from it. If you refine something such as a theory
            or idea, you improve it by making some alterations to it. People who
            are refined are very polite and well mannered.
          </p>
          <p>
            In the circular world, when anything is being refined, there is a
            process which it passes through, before becoming a refined product.
            For example, to refine crude oil for it to turn to petrol, it is not
            an hour job, even if it is, the crude oil itself would have
            undergone a lot of things before coming out to be a petroleum
            product. To refine things in this life, is not easy for non-living
            things, much more a living soul. In the process of refinement, it is
            neither easy for the refiner nor the refined, because a lot of
            things need to be put in or/and withdrawn in order for the beauty of
            the product to come out.
          </p>
          <p>
            Now let's talk about the refining process in the hand of the Lord in
            the life of the people. Refining process, what is it in the life of
            believers, the people of God?
          </p>
          <p>
            This is a process where God changes the life of his people. That is,
            the redeemed ones, to his own taste by removing all the vices that
            are not supposed to be there and adding all the spiritual fruits
            that are needed in their life.
          </p>
