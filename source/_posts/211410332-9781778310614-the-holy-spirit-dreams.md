          <p class="ql-align-justify">
            Often times, we become so eager and yearn to hear God speak to us on
            various aspects in our lives and situations that we find ourselves
            in. Sometimes, we become so desperate and expectant that we tend to
            focus on and create a fixed channel through which we want him to
            speak to us, and by so doing, we tend to miss His voice when he
            actually does speak.
          </p>
          <p class="ql-align-justify">
            It might not be a vision in a burning bush as with Moses, but the
            Holy Spirit does communicate to everyone in some way. Almost
            everybody dreams at least sometimes. The Holy Spirit communicates,
            especially to believers, through dreams and visions to enable them
            discover who they are and what they will become in the future.
          </p>
          <p class="ql-align-justify">
            We discover in the book of Acts in the New Testament that both God
            the Father and God the Holy Spirit communicated through dreams and
            visions. In this manner, the Holy Spirit provided guidance to
            countless people of the past.
          </p>
          <p class="ql-align-justify">
            Be informed that both dreams and visions are evidence of the
            outpouring of the Holy Spirit in the last days referred to in Acts
            2:17 to guide us. Don't take the warnings of dreams of the Holy
            Spirit for granted.
          </p>
          <p class="ql-align-justify">
            The Holy Spirit still communicates through dreams and visions to
            people today and His role in our dreams and visions is immeasurable.
            This book takes a look at some common dream imagery and explains how
            the Holy Spirit uses such imagery in dreams to warn, edify,
            encourage and comfort us.
          </p>
