          lectures at paranormal conventions. Clune lives in Southern
          California.<br />
          &nbsp;<br />
          Bob Davis is a commercial real estate investor by day and a paranormal
          researcher by night. Davis co-owns Planet Paranormal Radio, Planet
          Paranormal Investigations, and Queen Mary Shadows with partners Ash
          Blackwell and Brian Clune. Davis has been on more than thirty-five
          radio broadcasts and featured in several books and documentary films.
          He has been published in the <i>New York Daily News</i> and other
          publications, and has appeared on <i>Ghost Hunters</i>,
