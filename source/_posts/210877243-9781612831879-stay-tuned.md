          alternative treatments like chi gong and reiki sessions, Jenniffer
          reads Neale Donald Walsch, starts a spiritual diet plan, and uses the
          law of attraction to find free parking spaces. After his death, she

          celebrities, including conversations with don Miguel Ruiz, James Van
          Praagh, Caroline Myss, Deepak Chopra, and Russell Crowe. This is the
          funny, heart-breaking, and touching story of one skeptical
