          of hope and of beauty. Written by Aleister Crowley, Diary of a Drug
          Fiend tells the story of young Peter Pendragon and his lover Louise
          Laleham, and their adventures traveling through Europe in a cocaine
          and heroin haze. The bohemian couples' binges produce visions and
          poetic prophecies, but when their supply inevitably runs dry they find

          practical Magick to free themselves from addiction. Released in as his
          first published novel in 1922 and dubbed "a book for burning" by the
          papers of the time, Diary of a Drug Fiend reveals the poet, the lover,
          and the profound adept that was Aleister Crowley.
