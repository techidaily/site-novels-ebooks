        <p>
          Christopher Titmuss is the co-founder of one of the largest and most
          respected retreat centres in the UK, from where he travels all over
          the world to teach Awakening and Insight Meditation retreats. From
          1962 to 1967 he worked as a reporter and photojournalist in London,
          Turkey, Thailand, Laos and Australia - and then spent six years as a
          Buddhist monk in Thailand and India. For the last thirty years he has
          been teaching the Dharma. He is an active supporter of global
          responsibility and is a founder member of the twelve-strong
          international board of the Buddhist Peace Fellowship. In 1986 and 1992
          he stood for Parliament for the Green Party.
