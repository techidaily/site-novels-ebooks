            The Trigger Warnings of Caramelized Butterflies is a collection of
            poems with themes of trigger responses.

            dealing with addiction, experiencing an eating disorder/body

            domestic/emotional abuse, and falling in love.

            This collection is broken down into six parts: adolescence,

            ugly truth of coping with trauma.
