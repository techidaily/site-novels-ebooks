          <p>
            In this revolutionary treatise, J W Armstrong puts the compelling
            case that all diseases (except those caused by traumatism or
            structural disorders) can be cured by one simple means: urine
            therapy. The therapy is an entirely natural treatment, a drugless
            system of healing that treats the body as a whole. Moreover, the
            only ingredient needed is a substance manufactured in the body
            itself, rich in mineral salts, hormones and other vital substances,
            namely human urine. <br /><br />It may seem strange to take back
            into the body something that the body is apparently discarding. Yet
            the theory is similar to the natural practice of organic composting.
            Fallen leaves, when dug back into the soil, provide valuable mineral
            salts to nourish new plant life. The same principle holds true for
            the human body.
