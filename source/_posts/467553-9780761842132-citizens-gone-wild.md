          Citizens Gone Wild is a practical step-by-step guide for anyone
          interested in thinking for themselves. In this breakthrough book,
          author George Zilbergeld identifies, explains, and demonstrates twelve
          specific methods of analysis that anyone can use for critical thinking
          and decision making. As readers learn these methods they will also be
          learning skills useful in any profession and will gain the confidence
          to confront experts in any field. The methods presented here are easy
          to learn, powerful, and intuitive enough that using them will
          instantly instill the confidence needed to speak up. Tested over a
          period of twenty-five years, these methods have been used in a variety
          of classes and civic groups for professors, teachers, and activist
          citizens. Citizens Gone Wild can play an important role in empowering
          students and citizens by providing them with the right methods to
          think, decide, and speak-up for themselves.
