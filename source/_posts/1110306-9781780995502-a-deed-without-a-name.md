        <div accordion-content="" id="description">
          The field of witchcraft studies is continually over-turning new
          information and research about traditional witchcraft practices and
          their meanings. A Deed Without a Name seeks to weave together some of
          this cutting-edge research with insider information and practical
          know-how. Utilising her own decades of experience in witchcraft and
          core-shamanism Lee Morgan pulls together information from trial
          records, folklore and modern testimonials to deepen our understanding
          of the ecstatic and visionary substrata of Traditional Witchcraft.
          Those who identify themselves as 'Traditional' tend to read a lot of
          scholarly texts on the subject and yet still there remains a vast gulf
          between this information and knowledgeably applying it in practice;
          this book aims to close that gap. <br />, <br /><br />
