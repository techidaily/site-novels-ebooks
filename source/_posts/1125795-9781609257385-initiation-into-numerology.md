          Written for people who want to explore numbers, Johann Heyss brings
          together the symbology of number and the tarot so you can work with

          second name; he provides numbers that indicate professional talent,
          for understanding your sexuality, your lucky number, the three
          challenges, the life cycles -- including two basic cycles and the four
          pinnacles or seasons in life -- and the major arcana number in the
          tarot deck that best relates to your name. By using this system, you
          can see how numbers will affect your life and studies. Knowing your
          numbers will be beneficial when exploring the esoteric path, whether
          you are an initiate of the Western Mystery Tradition, magic, Wicca, or
