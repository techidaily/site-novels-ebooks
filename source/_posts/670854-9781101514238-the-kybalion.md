          It is one of the most mysterious and hotly debated occult works ever

          Initiates" since its first appearance in 1908. Debate rages over the
          identity of the Three Initiates, the origin of the book's mysterious
          title and insights, and the nature of the Hermetic teachings on which
          it is thought to be based. <br /><br />

          <i>The Kybalion: The Definitive Edition</i> presents the first full-
          scale analysis of this work of practical occult wisdom. It features
          the complete text of <i>The Kybalion</i> and the first-ever
          publication of Atkinson's previously unknown post-<i>Kybalion</i>
          work: <i>The Seven Cosmic Laws</i>. <br /><br />
          In an engaging introduction to this unique volume, religious scholar
          Philip Deslippe surveys the work's context, history, and impact
          (including as a source of spiritual insight to communities ranging
          from New Thought to Black Nationalism), and provides a biographical
          sketch of its elusive author, the New Thought pioneer William Walker
          Atkinson. <br /><br />
          As valuable to new fans as it is to longtime readers who crave more
          knowledge about Atkinson and his work,
          <i>The Kybalion: The Definitive Edition</i> illuminates the remarkable
          history of this long-cherished text.
